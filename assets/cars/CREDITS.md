# Car photo credits

Photos in this folder are used to represent vehicle listings in the inventory demo
(see `scripts/generate-inventory.js`). All are sourced from Wikimedia Commons under
free licenses (CC BY, CC BY-SA, or CC0) and resized to ~480px for use as thumbnails.
One photo is shared across all year/trim/color variations of the same brand+model
in the generated inventory — it is illustrative, not a photo of the specific unit.
